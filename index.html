Digital work registration

In the Internet era, information spreads very fast. Without pre registration, the probability of infringement of digital works is very high, and the cost of rights protection is very high. EVE trusted deposit service provides the ownership registration service of digital works, including text, code and other digital works, so that the original works can quickly generate electronic data deposit certificate and protect the copyright of digital works.

Service certificate

A large number of disputes over Internet services lie in whether the platform and businesses clearly inform users of service restrictive conditions in advance, including restrictive conditions, scope of use, service agreement, privacy protection terms, etc. EVE provides reliable timestamp, impartiality and neutrality, ensures recognition, and helps businesses or users safeguard their legitimate rights and interests.

Electronic voucher deposit

Electronic certificates, transaction vouchers, electronic contracts, electronic bills and other data vouchers are not easy to manage, easy to lose, difficult to circulate and difficult to verify. EVE can provide permanent deposit and verification services for electronic vouchers, which is not only convenient for management, but also easy to circulate.

Transaction deposit

There are a lot of detailed data in online shopping and trading behavior. These data will be easily lost over time, and it is difficult to connect them in series in case of disputes. EVE can provide fair and recognized certificate storage services for online transactions and double insurance for online data analysis.

Electronic data

EVE can provide SDK and embed it into the business system to obtain evidence of key electronic data in the business process. The evidence collection process is completed locally by the customer. At the same time, the evidence collection is stamped with authoritative time stamp and completed with digital signature. Under the condition of data privacy protection, the data ownership and generation time are confirmed.

Digital report

The certificate storage platform issues a certificate for each certificate storage record, indicating the ownership of the electronic data, the generation time and that it has not been tampered with after generation, displaying the certificate number, and supporting the verification of authenticity by scanning the QR code on the certificate.
